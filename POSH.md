# Question

### 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

**Ans -** 
* Physical contact or touchong.
* Sexual comments, joke or remarks.
* Explicit messages or images.
* Inappropriate gestures.
* Asking sexual favours in exchange of something.
* Any kind of verbal, non-verbal or pgysical conduct of sexuality or sexual nature.

### 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

**Ans -** 
* Make arrangements to keep myself and other remaining people safe from the accused.
* Document the incidents along with proper details and the proofs.
* Report about the incidents to my manager, HR or any other committie according to organisations policy.
* Provide any kind of supports to the victim and assure them about there privacy.
* Take help from victims and encourage them to report about incident by keeping the identity private.
* Full cooperation to the investigation of the incidents.
